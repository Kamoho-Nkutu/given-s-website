Product Detail

The Gilad Ultra Cotton T-shirt is crafted from substantial 6.0 oz./sq. yd. 100% ring-spun cotton fabric, offering exceptional softness and durability. Featuring a classic unisex fit with shoulder-to-shoulder taping and double-stitched seams, this tee provides superior comfort that lasts. The medium-weight fabric strikes the perfect balance - substantial enough to prevent transparency yet lightweight for all-day wear. The tear-away label eliminates neck irritation while maintaining a clean, professional look.
